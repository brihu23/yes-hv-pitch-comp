General info & timeline

The Yale x Harvard Pitch Competition will be run through OnePager. Please indicate interest as early as Sunday, March 21st at 12:00 am EST and as late as Monday, April 5th at 11:59 pm EST. After you indicate interest, we’ll help you in crafting the perfect OnePager by providing resources, support, and feedback. Final OnePagers are due by Monday, April 12th at 11:59 pm EST. Our judging period will take place between April 14th and April 28th, and selected finalists will be asked to pitch on Friday, April 30th at 7:30 pm EST.

Regardless of whether you are selected as a finalist, you may choose to share your OnePager with our network of Yale and Harvard alumni angel investors along with our venture capital community. This means that your start-up will be viewed by Yale and Harvard alumni irrespective of pitch competition outcomes—this grants you access to a friendly community of angels who are looking to connect and support your start-up.

Introduction to OnePager

OnePager is a tool for founders to consolidate and share their startup’s information. With OnePager, founders can bring together their company overview, team info, pitch deck, demo video, FAQs, and more under an easily shared URL with privacy settings and analytics built in. OnePagers are used by founders for sharing new projects, fundraising, sales, and recruiting. Currently, founders have to use static PDFs, limited smart link tools, or spend days building a new landing page to share important information with external stakeholders. Ultimately, OnePager is a storytelling tool for founders that enables them to better communicate who they are, what they're building, where they’re headed, and what they need.
